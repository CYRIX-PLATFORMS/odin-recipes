Water fufu and Eru

Eru is a dish prepared in Cameroon and is a very popular one at that in all her regions.

This recipe will cover all the ingredients and procedure required in order to successfully arrive at this delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Eru leaves
  • Water leaves
  • Maggi
  • salt
  • palm oil
  • Dry fish
  • Meat
  • canda

Steps

  1. Wash the water leaf
  2. After washing, we slice it. Hygeine is very important.
  3. Stand a pot on the fire and boil place the sliced and washed water leaf and eru leaves inside.
  4. Put all your ingredients except the palm oil for now.
    • Dry fish
    • Pepper
    • Maggi
    • Salt
  5. Allow the food to cook with the ingredients added then add the appropiate amount of palm oil and allow it to cook.
  6. That will be all about the eru
